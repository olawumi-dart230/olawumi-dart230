Love is all I need

By, Olawumi Hassan


Recent years of not knowing the true definition of love,
Because you never had a view of what true love is.
Living with a divored parent trying to figure out
How her failed marriage won't influence her daughter's view of love.
Waking up every morning praying to find love myself,
After having my heart broken for the past two years.
Having the thought that you've finally found someone
Who actually loves you, and you're filled with joy
Telling all yourfriends that you have met the one.
Just to find out days later he isn't what you think he is.
Waking up asking yourself why doesn't nothing work out for you.
But praying that one day you will have what you deserve.
